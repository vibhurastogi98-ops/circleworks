# Privacy Policy

**Last updated**: May 23, 2026

## Data We Collect

We collect account details, company information, administrator details, billing records, product usage events, support messages, device metadata, and security logs.

When customers use CircleWorks for payroll and HR operations, we may process employee and contractor profile data, compensation details, tax identifiers, bank account information, benefits elections, time records, documents, and compliance records.

## How We Use It

We use data to provide and secure the services, process payroll, maintain HR records, administer benefits, support customers, improve product functionality, prevent fraud, satisfy legal obligations, and communicate important service updates.

We do not sell employee payroll or HR data. We do not use customer employee records to train foundation models.

## Who We Share With

We share information only as needed with service providers, subprocessors, payment processors, banking partners, benefits providers, tax agencies, compliance partners, professional advisors, and authorities where legally required.

These parties are expected to process information under appropriate confidentiality, security, and purpose limitations.

## Your Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to request access, correction, deletion, portability, restriction, or objection concerning your personal information.

To exercise these rights, contact privacy@circleworks.com or use the Data Subject Request form linked on this page.

## California CCPA Rights

California residents may request to know, access, correct, or delete covered personal information. California residents may also opt out of the sale or sharing of personal information where applicable.

CircleWorks uses an opt-out model for California users for non-essential analytics, marketing, and personalization cookies. Rejecting non-essential cookies or disabling those categories in Cookie Preferences records that opt-out choice.

[Submit a Data Subject Request](/contact?topic=data-subject-request)

## Data Retention

We retain information for as long as needed to provide the services, comply with payroll, tax, accounting, employment, legal, audit, and security obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements.

Payroll and tax records may be retained for legally required periods. Security logs and product telemetry are retained according to operational need and then deleted or de-identified.

## Security

CircleWorks uses administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards designed to protect personal information, including encryption in transit, encryption at rest, access controls, monitoring, and vendor review.

No security program can guarantee absolute protection, but we work to maintain safeguards appropriate to the sensitivity of payroll and HR data.

## Cookies

CircleWorks uses essential cookies for authentication, security, consent storage, and core product functionality. With consent, we may use analytics, marketing, and personalization cookies.

You can manage non-essential cookies through the cookie banner or Cookie Policy page.

## Contact

Questions about this Privacy Policy or privacy rights requests may be sent to privacy@circleworks.com.
