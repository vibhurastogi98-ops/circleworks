# Privacy Policy

**Last updated**: April 5, 2026

## 1. Data We Collect
We collect information that you manually provide to us when registering for our service, such as your name, corporate email address, encrypted password, and company details. In fulfilling payroll automation, we process highly sensitive data belonging to your employees, including SSNs, bank details, and compensation packages. We also automatically log your IP address, browser type, and interactions on our platform for auditing and security purposes.

## 2. How We Use It
Your data is used strictly to provide the core functionalities of CircleWorks: processing payroll accurately, maintaining HR compliance records, generating required tax filings, and validating SSO authentication. We do not use user or employee data to train our foundational language models, nor do we sell metadata.

## 3. Who We Share With
We only share information with certified subprocessors required to maintain our infrastructure. This includes robust, SOC 2 Type II certified cloud providers (AWS, Cloudflare), mandated state and federal tax agencies, and banking partners (Stripe, Plaid) required to physically transit funds. We do not sell your personal data to ad networks or external parties.

## 4. Your Rights
Under global privacy standards, you have the right to request access to the personal data we hold about you, request corrections to inaccurate entries, or formally request erasure of your data from our active systems. 

## 5. California CCPA Rights
If you are a California resident, the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) provides you with additional rights regarding your data. You may request disclosure showing what specific categories of your data we have collected over the prior 12-month period. To exercise a [Data Subject Request](mailto:privacy@circleworks.com), please click the link below or contact us via email.

## 6. Data Retention
We retain corporate tax and payroll records for a minimum of 7 years as mandated by federal IRS legislation. After internal retention limits expire, records are securely scrubbed from both primary databases and encrypted cold-storage backups. Application activity logs are purged after 90 days.

## 7. Security
All data transmitted between your browser and our infrastructure is secured using modern TLS 1.3 encryption. Data at rest is encrypted using AES-256 standards with rotating hardware keys. Multi-factor authentication is enforced administratively on our internal personnel.

## 8. Cookies
We utilize cookies primarily to sustain strict session security (Essential Cookies). You may optionally consent to Analytics or Marketing cookies. More details can be found in our comprehensive Cookie Policy.

## 9. Contact
If you have questions, please direct them to privacy@circleworks.com or mail our physical headquarters.
