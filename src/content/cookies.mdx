## Managing Preferences

Essential cookies are always on because CircleWorks needs them for security, authentication, consent records, and core functionality.

Analytics, marketing, and personalization cookies are optional. You can accept all, reject non-essential cookies, or manage category preferences through the cookie banner.

## California Opt-Out

For California users, rejecting non-essential cookies or turning off marketing and analytics categories records an opt-out choice for applicable cookie-based sale or sharing activity.
